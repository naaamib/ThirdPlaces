    What is a "Third Place"?

    A third place is a location for people to socialize and exist outside of outside of work and home. Typically these places consist of malls, cafes, and bookstores, but as a cosequence of an anti-youth culture, a global pandemic and late stage capatalism, these places are hard to come by. Nowadays many people spend their day-to-day in an endless cylce of going to work, then home, then back to work which has resulted in what the surgeon general refers to as a pandemic of loniliness. The lack of third places has resulted in a lack of socialization which has real adverse health effects.
